About Dahilayan Hotel

Where luxury meets natural beauty in the heart of Bukidnon

Our Story

Established in 2010, Dahilayan Hotel emerged from a vision to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience the breathtaking beauty of the Philippine highlands while enjoying world-class comfort and service.

Founded by the Mendoza family, who have deep roots in Bukidnon province, the hotel was designed to showcase the region's natural splendor while respecting and preserving its ecological balance. What began as a small 15-room lodge has evolved into one of the region's premier luxury destinations.

Today, Dahilayan Hotel stands as a testament to sustainable luxury, seamlessly blending modern amenities with traditional Filipino hospitality and a deep respect for our natural surroundings.

Our Mission

To provide our guests with an unforgettable mountain retreat experience, combining luxurious comfort with authentic Filipino hospitality while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit the local community and preserve our natural environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier mountain resort destination in the Philippines, known for exceptional service, responsible tourism, and creating meaningful connections between our guests and the natural beauty of Bukidnon.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to environmentally responsible practices.
  • Community: We support and engage with our local community.
  • Authenticity: We celebrate and share the rich cultural heritage of our region.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek to improve and evolve our offerings.

Hotel Features & Amenities

Mountain View Restaurant

Our signature restaurant serves local and international cuisine using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Enjoy panoramic views while dining on expertly prepared dishes.

Highlands Spa

Indulge in rejuvenating treatments inspired by traditional Filipino healing practices, using organic products sourced from the mountains.

Infinity Pool

Our heated infinity pool offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, creating the perfect setting for relaxation.

Adventure Center

Explore the natural wonders of Dahilayan with guided hikes, mountain biking, and adventure activities arranged by our experienced staff.

Sky Lounge

Enjoy signature cocktails and panoramic sunset views at our rooftop lounge, featuring live music on weekends.

Conference Facilities

Modern meeting rooms and event spaces with breathtaking backdrops, perfect for corporate retreats, weddings, and special celebrations.
